Race
Race is an event type in Need for Speed: Heat, made up of either multiple lap circuits or single point-to-point sprints, and take place on roads. The first racer to cross the finish line after completing all laps or reaching the end point of a sprint first is the winner.

Every route has multiple checkpoints that racers must pass through in order and they all have a recommended road-grip handling profile.

An event can take place during the day or at night:

  • Day - Players earn bank (Bank) from participating in events hosted by the Speedhunters Showdown and police will not get involved.
  • Night - Players earn rep (Rep) from participating in illicit street racing events and engaging with the PCPD.
Drift
Drift Trial is an event type in Need for Speed: Heat, made up of either multiple lap circuits or single point-to-point sprints, and take place on roads.

The player has to beat a score target before they complete all laps of a circuit or reach the end of a point-to-point sprint.

Every route has multiple checkpoints that players must pass through in order and they all have a recommended road-drift handling profile.

An event can take place during the day or at night:

  • Day - Players earn bank (Bank) from participating in events hosted by the Speedhunters Showdown and police will not get involved.
  • Night - Players earn rep (Rep) from participating in illicit street racing events and engaging with the PCPD.
Customization
  • PERFORMANCE CUSTOMIZATION
    weak your car’s performance to match your driving preference, whatever your style. There are four main areas you can customize: Engine, Chassis, Drivetrain, and Auxiliary. Upgrading the Engine will give you more power to play with. But if you’re all about how your ride handles you’ll want to focus on the Chassis. Drivetrain gives you access to a combination of handling parts and pure performance parts. And Auxiliary covers everything from nitrous to repair kits.

  • VISUAL CUSTOMIZATION
    If you can dream it, you can design it in Need for Speed Heat. You’ve got a massive assortment of paint and finish options to choose from, not to mention the hottest splitters, bumpers, tires, grilles, spoilers, and morel. Wild customs? Go faster stripes? It’s all you. Looking for even more options? The wrap editor is back and better than ever, with new decals, collectibles, and cop logos!

  • Character Customization
    For the first time in Need for Speed history customization doesn’t begin and end on four wheels. Now you can customize your character to fit your mood, your style, or to match the look and feel of your ride.
